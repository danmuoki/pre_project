Speciality Drinks

Zobo

Found in every street corner across Nigeria, Zobo (dry hibiscus flower petals) is a refreshing drink infused with pineapples, ginger, and honey. It has some surprisingly great health benefits such as regulating blood pressure, preventing infection, and increasing fertility in women. A great contender for Ginger Dawa. Enjoy Zobo worm or cold.

Pineapple Mint Juice

Served in tall, chilled glass, Mama Ashanti’s refreshing, thirst quenching, health and delicious Pineapples Mint Juice is a must-have every time you visit. The sweet yet acidic taste of fresh pineapples combines perfectly with the tingles of mint. The powerful combo is a nutritional hero, chock full of antioxidants and immunity-boosting properties. Order a glass full with your next meal.

Long Island Cocktails

A boozy delight of a seven deadly sins! This stalwart mix of vodka, gin, tequila, rum, triple sec, and coke is sticky-sweet-delicious and dangerous! Mama Ashanti’s Long Island blend has a special secret ingredient familiar only to loyal fans, making it a staple at every dinner table at Mama Ashanti Lavington or Mama Ashanti Waterfront. Want to get wild tonight? This Long Island is rich with wild potential.
